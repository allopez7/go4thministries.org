Welcome to Go Forth Ministries

Jesus Christ called us to preach the gospel to all nations. Go Forth Ministries has taken his word to heart and has over the past 30 years travelled throughout the world preaching and making disciples of all nations.

Our Church

Evangelist Neil Sundblad founded Go Forth Ministries over 30 years ago. He has travelled many nations and preached the gospel to the lost.

Our History

Evangelist Neil Sundblad was delivered from drugs and addiction. He was radically set free and began to preach what God had done in his life.
